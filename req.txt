PROJECT REQUIREMENTS (CLIENT CONTENT CHECKLIST)
Construction Company Website

Purpose
- Build a professional, modern website to present your construction company, showcase services and projects, and generate leads via contact/quote inquiries.

Client-Provided Content (Required)
1) Company identity
   - Company name (exact spelling)
   - Company logo (preferred formats: SVG/PNG; transparent background if possible)
   - Brand colors (if any) and preferred font style (optional)

2) Company overview
   - Short description / tagline (1–2 lines)
   - Detailed “About the Company” content including:
     - Year established / company history
     - Vision / mission
     - What the company brings to the community (values/impact)

3) Services
   - List of all services you offer
   - Short description for each service (2–5 lines each)
   - (Optional) Key features/benefits per service (3–6 bullet points)

4) Projects / Portfolio
   - Projects list with details for each project:
     - Project title
     - Location (city/area)
     - Project type/category: Residential / Commercial / Industrial / Renovation
     - Short description (3–6 lines)
     - (Optional) Duration / completion date
   - Project images (high-resolution; ideally 3–8 images per project)
   - Confirmation that you own the rights to use these images on the website

5) Company stats (for homepage/credibility section)
   - Years of experience
   - Completed projects (count)
   - Satisfied clients (count)
   - Team members (count)

6) Contact information
   - Office address
   - Phone number(s)
   - Email address
   - Working hours (days & times)
   - (Optional) Google Maps link / coordinates

7) Website imagery
   - Office/landscape/hero image (wide, high quality)
   - (Optional) Additional brand photography for banners/sections

Optional (Recommended)
- Testimonials (client name, rating, short review, company/project type if applicable)
- Social media links (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.)
- FAQs (5–10 common questions and answers)
- Legal pages (Privacy Policy / Terms) if needed

Notes / Assumptions
- All content should be provided in editable text (Word/Google Doc) and images in original quality.
- If content/images are not available, placeholder content may be used temporarily until final assets are received.
